What is the Dark web and the Deep web?

We have all heard of the dark web and the dark web. But what does it mean in your head? Take a second to think about it.

There are many misconceptions about the dark web and the deep web. Continue on to find out!

The surface web

Many of us, like you and me are on the surface web, doing different things like reading this blog, on youtube, watching anime, and etc. It is one of the places to go to online when you are bored at home. Either doing your work or just chilling. The Surface Web only consists of 10 percent of the information that is on the internet. The Surface Web is made with a collection of public Web pages on a server accessible by any search engine.

The deep web

The deep web is different from the Dark web! People get it confused all the time! It is completely different! The deep web refers to all the pages which are not indexed. Meaining that most search engines won’t return them to you after a search. It is also a place where people's informations are stored securely and legally. Things such as academic records, doctors' informations, banks and etc. In order to access one of these pages, you need to either know their exact link or you clicking on another link once you get in the deep side of the web. The deep web can also include significant parts of the legitimate, mainstream web (like Netflix or shopping websites), simply because they are personalized for users and not all URLs are meant to be the same link.

The dark web

The dark web is a layer even deeper- technically also part of the deep web, but more deep. The dark web is a place where you don't want to be. This place is full of illegal activities and trafficking. There is no way to get there on a surface web. However, web browsers like Tor allows you to access with no difficulties. Tor allows you to hide your ip addresses most of the time. But there are always people who are better than the software you are using.

Conclusion

A lot of times, these two terms gets used confusingly, but the deep web just refers to non-recorded pages, while the dark web refers to pages which are both non-indexed and involved in illegal activites. The iceberg in the beginning is used as a metaphor to show how deep the dark web is. Despite their names, always remember that the dark web is deeper than the deep web. The deep web is generally neutral, it can be used for good or for bad. The dark web is where the people who lack morals come together.
